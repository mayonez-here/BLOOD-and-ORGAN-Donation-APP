Welcome to blood and organ donation portal. Our main aim is to mobilize people all over Kenya to come up and pledge organs and blood by registering as donors. Blood and organs are crucial components in our bodies that guarantee survival. We can save a lot of lives when we donate blood and organs to those in need of them.

Did you know?

1)Donating blood saves life and improves health.
2) The amount of blood in an average adult is 10 pints.
3) The demand for blood in Kenya is currently 200,000 units a year against a collection of 130,000 units leaving a shortfall of 70,000 units a year.
4)There is no substitute for human blood

Donation Regions in Kenya

Donation Regions(Regional Transfusion Centers) in Kenya include Nairobi, Mombasa, Embu, Nakuru, Eldoret and Kisumu. There are 14 satellite centers located in Machakos, Kisii, Voi, Meru, Naivasha, Kakamega, Kericho, Nyeri, Garissa, Malindi, Thika, Lodwar, Bungoma and Kitale.
